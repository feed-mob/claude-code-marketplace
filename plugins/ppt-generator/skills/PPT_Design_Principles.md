## ðŸ’¡ 3. Presentation Principles

1.  **Audience-Centered**
    -   Adapt tone and content to your audience (executives, clients,
        peers).
    -   Focus on their needs, not just your data.
2.  **Speaker-Led Delivery**
    -   The PPT supports your talk---it's not your script.
    -   Keep slides minimal and elaborate verbally.
3.  **Animations & Transitions**
    -   Use subtle animations to guide attention.
    -   Avoid flashy or distracting effects.
